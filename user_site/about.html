About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the journey that brought us here

Our Mission

To foster unity among Madi students at Makerere University, promote academic excellence, provide a platform for personal and professional development, and advocate for student rights and welfare while maintaining strong cultural identity and values.

Our Vision

To be the leading student association that empowers Madi students to achieve academic excellence, develop leadership skills, and contribute meaningfully to society while preserving and promoting our rich cultural heritage.

Our History

A journey of growth, unity, and excellence

2009

Foundation

The Madi Makerere University Students Association was founded by a group of dedicated students with the vision of creating a supportive community for Madi students.

2012

First Major Event

Organized our first cultural week celebration, bringing together over 200 students and showcasing Madi culture to the university community.

2015

Academic Excellence Program

Launched our mentorship program connecting senior students with freshmen, resulting in improved academic performance across all departments.

2018

Community Outreach

Initiated community service projects in Madi communities, providing educational support and resources to local schools.

2021

Digital Transformation

Adapted to online learning and virtual events during the pandemic, maintaining community connections through innovative digital platforms.

2024

New Website Launch

Launched our comprehensive website to better serve our members and provide easy access to information and resources.

Our Objectives

Goals that drive our mission forward

Academic Excellence

Promote academic achievement through study groups, mentorship programs, and academic support services.

Community Building

Foster unity and friendship among Madi students through social events, cultural activities, and networking opportunities.

Student Advocacy

Represent student interests and advocate for their rights and welfare within the university and beyond.

Cultural Preservation

Preserve and promote Madi culture, traditions, and values through cultural events and educational programs.

Leadership Development

Develop leadership skills among members through training programs, workshops, and leadership opportunities.

Community Service

Engage in community service projects that benefit society and contribute to national development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I become a member?

To become a member, simply visit our office during office hours, fill out the membership form, and pay the annual membership fee. You can also contact us through our website or social media for more information.

What services do you offer?

We offer academic support, student welfare services, career guidance, social activities, and student advocacy. Visit our services page for detailed information about each service.

How can I get involved in leadership?

We hold annual elections for leadership positions. You can also join various committees and departments. Contact our leadership team or visit our leadership page for more information about available opportunities.

Do you offer financial assistance?

Yes, we provide information about scholarships, bursaries, and other financial aid opportunities. We also offer emergency financial support for members in need. Contact our welfare department for more information.

How often do you hold events?

We organize events throughout the academic year, including cultural celebrations, academic workshops, sports tournaments, and social gatherings. Check our events page for the latest schedule.
